League Information

La Mirada Adult Basketball will once again create a recreational basketball league in our area. The league is designed to provide men and women an outlet to compete, but more importantly help them to develop their skills and give all participants an equal opportunity to play. To accomplish this, we have instituted rules that require equal play. The program is designed to be competitive and each game will have a score as well as an end of season March Maddness Tournament.
